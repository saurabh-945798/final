"Robo War" is an exciting and innovative event that brings together enthusiasts of robotics, engineering, and technology to showcase their creativity, skills, and passion for automation. This thrilling competition challenges participants to design, build, and program robots to engage in captivating battles and face-offs.

Objective: The primary objective of Robo-War is to design a robot that can effectively navigate and defeat opponents in a controlled environment. This requires participants to develop strong programming and engineering skills, as well as strategic thinking and problem-solving abilities.
